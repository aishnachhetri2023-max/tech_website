Welcome to My Textile Journey

Welcome to my textile journey! What started as a curiosity has blossomed into a true passion. From learning the basics of fabrics and techniques to completing my first project, each step has taught me new skills and expanded my creativity. Join me as I continue to explore the art of textiles!

My Textile Story

It all started with zero knowledge about fabrics, needles, and threads. But after joining a textile group at school, I discovered a world I didn't know existed. The projects I've worked on have not only helped me develop technical skills but have also given me the confidence to create and experiment with different materials, patterns, and designs. Each project is an opportunity to learn and grow in this incredible field.
